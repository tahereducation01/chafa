About Chafa

Our Story

Pune's Premier Health-Conscious Destination

Founded with a vision to create a sanctuary for mindful living, Chafa Cafe & Studio brings together wholesome plant-based cuisine and holistic wellness under one roof. Our journey began with a simple belief: nourishing food and mindful movement are the cornerstones of a vibrant life.

As a 100% pure vegetarian establishment, we celebrate the richness of plant-based ingredients, transforming them into culinary masterpieces that delight both the palate and the soul. Our chefs combine global culinary techniques with locally-sourced, organic produce to create dishes that are as nutritious as they are delicious.

The Chafa Philosophy

Nourish Body & Soul

Mindful nourishment without compromise

At Chafa, we believe that what you consume should make you feel vibrant, energized, and satisfied. Our chefs and nutritionists collaborate to create globally-inspired dishes that are art on a plate and fuel for your body.

We source local, organic ingredients to craft meals that support various lifestyles while staying true to our pure vegetarian ethos.
